Hello!

I am a senior at Haverford College, studying Astrophysics with a concentration in Scientific Computing. I use pulsar timing data from NICER to model the pulse-to-pulse variation in the X-ray emission of the Crab Pulsar with Dr. Andrea Lommen. I am also working with Dr. Meng Sun, Dr. Vicky Kalogera, and Dr. Zoheyr Doctor at Northwestern University to apply a new mechanism of wind mass-transfer to low-mass binary systems using the detailed binary evolution codes MESA and POSYDON.

My primary research interests center around gravitational wave and multi-messenger astronomy, black holes, and neutron stars. I particularly enjoy computational astrophysics, and I am currently applying to PhD programs in Astronomy.
